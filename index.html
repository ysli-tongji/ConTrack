ConTrack

Analysis of pavement deterioration is critical for road maintenance. Many section-based pavement performance evaluation methodologies have been investigated to determine the deteriorating tendency from a macro perspective. However, little research shed light on the refined deterioration analysis for single distress, which is valuable for daily and preventive maintenance. This dataset is collected from over 270 kilometers of Chinese urban roads. It records the continuous tracking process of each distress. Meteorological information is also recorded. This dataset can be used not only to analyze the deterioration of the distress, but also a good choice for training the distress detection algorithm.
